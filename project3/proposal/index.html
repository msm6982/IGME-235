Miles Michell: Project 3 Concept

Working Title: Night Flight

Pitch

It's a 2D arcade endless survival game where you play as a bat who has a bit for trouble flying. Your objective is to survive the night collecting as much collectable fruit as possible while avoiding obstacle and enemy owls. You can control the direction of the bat but the only way to gain forward momentum is to flap your wings a predetermined distance, because you're a bad flyer you might have to do this a lot to stay afloat!

Genre

Comparable Genres would definitely be Arcade and High Score Survival

Platform

Focusing on developing for desktop Web but mobile could work, tapping the screen to flap might be much more uncomfortable

Story

The bulk of the story is in the pitch so it is a fairly simple survival of the fittest narrative, but much more tongue and cheek, which is complemented by its aesthetics

Aesthetics

Due to it's easy pick up and go gameplay, the aesthetics would be tongue and cheek and cartoony. I will rely on open source art and my crude drawings to achieve this. Source I'll most definitely use: https://opengameart.org/

Game Play

The main controls are the turning of the bats direction with the left and right arrow keys and the flap that propels them forward against gravity but in a predefined length. Think Flappy Bird but less difficult in controls. The main objective is to gain points by flying the highest and to collect collectible food to further increase their score. Obstacles in the form of enemy AI Owls, Branches and Trees, and gravity itself are what stand in your way of getting the highest score.
