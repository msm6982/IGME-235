Night Flight

Take Flight and Survive the Night!

You are a bat who does not really have the hang of flying, and thus you struggle to remain airborne. Regardless you must fly into the night to eat, but you are not alone

Instructions

  • Don't crash on the ground
  • Eat fireflies to increase your score
  • Avoid enemey owls who want to eat you
  • Eating enough fireflies can increase your health but makes owls hungrier

Controls

  • Press Spacebar to propel foward
  • Hold 'A' and 'D' to rotate left and right
